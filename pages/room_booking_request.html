JRMSU TC Boarding House Rules and Regulations

1. Payment Terms: Monthly rent is due on the 1st of each month. Late payments incur a ₱100 penalty per day.

2. Occupancy Rules: Maximum occupancy per room must be strictly observed. Unauthorized guests are not allowed overnight.

3. Quiet Hours: Quiet hours are from 10:00 PM to 6:00 AM. Loud music, parties, or disruptive behavior is prohibited.

4. Cleanliness: Students are responsible for maintaining cleanliness in their rooms and common areas.

5. Damage Policy: Students are liable for any damage to property beyond normal wear and tear.

6. Visitors: All visitors must be registered at the front desk and are allowed only during designated hours (8:00 AM - 8:00 PM).

7. Security: Students must always carry their boarding house ID and are responsible for their personal belongings.

8. Prohibited Items: Alcoholic beverages, illegal drugs, weapons, and cooking appliances are strictly prohibited.

9. Termination: Violation of rules may result in immediate termination of boarding house privileges without refund.

10. Emergency Procedures: Students must familiarize themselves with emergency exits and procedures.
